How to sign up for MTN MTN WakaNet Fiber

Phone

  • Download MyMTN app from Google and Apple store
  • Go to MyMTN app, select Buy and then MTN WakaNet Fiber
  • Check for WakaNet Fiber availability.
  • Enter your location in the map and confirm then proceed to make payments.

Website

  • www.mtn.co.ug/wakanet
  • Pin your location to confirm availability and make payment.

To renew your subscription
